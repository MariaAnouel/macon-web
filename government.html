Macon County Government

Board of Commissioners

Macon County is governed by an elected Board of Commissioners, responsible for setting policies and overseeing the administration of county services. The Board works closely with the County Manager to implement decisions and manage daily operations.

Departments & Services

  • Department of Social Services: Provides assistance programs, including food and nutrition services, Medicaid, and child welfare services.
    1832 Lakeside Drive, Franklin, NC 28734 – (828) 349-2124
  • Planning & Code Enforcement: Oversees building inspections, environmental services, and county planning initiatives.
    (828) 349-2073
  • Register of Deeds: Manages public records, including deeds, marriage licenses, and vital records.
    5 West Main Street, Franklin, NC 28734 – (828) 349-2097

Public Safety

The Macon County Sheriff's Office provides law enforcement services, while Emergency Services coordinate 911 communications, EMS, and fire marshal duties to ensure community safety.
