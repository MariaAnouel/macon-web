Welcome to the Official Website of Macon County, North Carolina

Macon County Government is dedicated to serving our growing community.

As one of the fastest growing counties in North Carolina, Macon County strives to serve our citizens, business community and those visiting the area with the most up-to-date resources possible.

This online portal to Macon County North Carolina Government will allow you to learn more about our agencies, download an official form, search for maps, open a business or plan your visit.
